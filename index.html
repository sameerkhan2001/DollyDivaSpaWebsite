About Me

Director/Owner

Hi my name is Tina and I’m the owner of Dolly Diva Spa. I’m excited to launch my new business, offering mobile services. I have over 20 years experience in the medical field. I started in my early 20’s with medical administration and eventually continued my education and became a doctors assistant. I loved working in this field and with the team I was with but it was time for me to branch out on my own and try something that I know I was meant to do.

Dolly Diva Spa is a mobile service (I come to your home) offering all the services mentioned in my website. Amid this COVID-19 pandemic I realized that mobile is the way to promote these services to my clients needs, in a safe environment and more comfortable atmosphere. For me it’s not the financial aspect of my new business, it’s knowing that my clients can book an appointment that fits their schedule in their own home where they feel most comfortable. I supply everything from spa bed to spa treatment machine/ apparel/ masks, gloves, sanitizer etc. I provide the safest way to receive all your beauty needs in the comfort of your own home.
